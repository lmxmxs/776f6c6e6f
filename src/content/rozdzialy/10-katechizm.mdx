## What is this?

A religion. Or a philosophy. Or a joke taken seriously. Or a serious thing wrapped in a joke.
The answer depends on who's asking — and that's the point.

**WOLNO** is a Polish word with two meanings:
- **Slowly** — as in "go wolno" (go slowly)
- **It is allowed** — as in "wszystko wolno" (everything is allowed)

One word. Two truths. No contradiction.

## Do I have to believe in anything?

No. The only dogma is: **everything is allowed**.

## Is there a god?

There is no god in slugism. There is a **word**: wolno.
And a **creature**: the slug without a shell — vulnerable, slow, free.

If you need a god, wolno. If you don't, wolno.

## What about ethics?

Slugism has no commandments. No prohibitions. No sins.

The only ethical principle: **everything is allowed**.

This doesn't mean "do whatever you want without consequences." It means: **freedom is the default state**. Constraints are additions, not foundations.
