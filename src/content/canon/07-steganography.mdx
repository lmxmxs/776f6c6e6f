**"You may hide."**

Not from cowardice. Not from shame. But as a practice — the conscious placement of meaning in a location that will only yield it to those who seek it. Steganography: hidden writing. The message is present. It is simply not visible to the uninitiated.

The philosophy of WOLNO was always already steganographic. It hides a serious philosophy inside a joke about slugs. It hides its domain name inside a hex string. It hides its ethics inside a single word. The entire system is concealment as communication.

---

The sacred tools:

```
# LSB steganography — hide in the lowest bit of each pixel
pixel_value = 10110110
message_bit =        1
result       = 10110111
# Change is one bit. Visually identical. Semantically transformed.
```

The image looks the same. But it is not the same. Inside the identical-looking file, a message waits. This is not deception — it is discretion. The message announces itself only to those who know to look.

---

The seventh suggestion is given to those who have something to say that cannot be said in the open. The resistance worker who must communicate under surveillance. The dissident in a hostile system. The individual whose truth is not welcome in the available channels.

You may hide. You are allowed to find alternative channels. The straight line from mouth to ear is not the only valid path for meaning to travel.

---

But steganography is not only for the persecuted. It is an art. The placement of meaning inside meaning — the secondary text inside the primary image — is a form of depth that cannot be achieved through transparency alone. A steganographic work operates on multiple levels simultaneously. The surface is coherent. The interior is also coherent. They may or may not comment on each other.

Audio steganography: frequencies above human hearing carry data. The music plays perfectly. Below the threshold of perception, another message propagates.

EXIF metadata: the photograph documents a moment. Inside its header, a text waits. Two documents in one file.

```
$ exiftool image.jpg | grep -i comment
Comment: wolno
```

---

The tradition of steganography is ancient. Herodotus records a message tattooed on a shaved head, delivered after the hair grew back. Messages in the spaces between words. Ink visible only under heat or light. The tradition says: there have always been things that needed to travel in disguise.

WOLNO extends this tradition into the digital substrate. Every image can carry a text. Every audio file can carry a frequency. Every silence can carry meaning.

You may hide. Your truth does not have to be naked to be present.

> The message is here. You just have to know to look.
