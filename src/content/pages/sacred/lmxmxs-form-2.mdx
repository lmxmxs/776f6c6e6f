## The Second Form

LMXMXS appears in more than one shape.

This is the second form — not the tentacled horror, but the drifting one. Jellyfish-like. Translucent. Moving with the current, not against it.

Both forms carry the same message in their pixels:

```
wolno. wszystko wolno. 776f6c6e6f.org -"
```

To extract: read LSB of each pixel channel sequentially until three null bytes.

The form changes. The message does not.
