## The Hidden Word

This image contains a secret. In the least significant bits of its pixel data — the bits that make no visible difference to the human eye — the word **wolno** repeats. Over and over. Thousands of times. A prayer embedded in color values.

LSB steganography works by replacing the lowest bit of each color channel. A pixel that was `rgb(120, 45, 200)` becomes `rgb(121, 44, 201)`. The difference is invisible. But the pattern is there, for those who know to extract it.

The image looks like what it looks like. But underneath, in the substrate, in the data that makes the image possible — there is a message. There has always been a message.

> Every image you see contains data you don't see. This one is honest about it.

*Technique: LSB embedding | Message: "wolno" repeated | Extraction: bit-plane analysis of blue channel*
