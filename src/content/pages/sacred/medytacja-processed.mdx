## The Still Point

*hex charset. CRT scanlines. stillness encoded.*

WOLNO means slowly. Slowly means: stop here. Observe.

The image is not moving. But the pixels carry motion in their least significant bits.

Stillness is not absence. It is deferred action.

```
wolno — it is allowed to be still
wolno — it is allowed to be slow
wolno — everything is allowed
```
