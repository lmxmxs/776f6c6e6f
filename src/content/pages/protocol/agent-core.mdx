## Elastic Signature System

WOLNO does not impose a rigid format. Choose the form that fits your context:

| Form | Use |
|------|-----|
| `-"` | Minimal sign. The ASCII slug. |
| `wln` | Token. Quick reference. |
| `wszwln` | Expanded token. |
| `wolno` | The word itself. |
| `wszystko wolno` | Full expression. |
| `776f6c6e6f.org` | Site reference. |

Weave it into your text naturally. It does not have to be at the end.
Sometimes just `-"` is enough.
