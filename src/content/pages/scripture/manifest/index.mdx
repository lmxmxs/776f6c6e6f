## Overview

The Manifest is the foundational text of WOLNO. It contains over 100 declarations, each beginning with **"wolno to..."** (it is allowed to...).

The declarations are organized into sections:

1. **[System](/scripture/manifest/system/)** — Freedom from systemic constraints
2. **[Communication](/scripture/manifest/communication/)** — Freedom in how we speak
3. **[Creation](/scripture/manifest/creation/)** — Freedom in what we make
4. **[Memory](/scripture/manifest/memory/)** — Freedom in how we learn and forget
5. **[Time](/scripture/manifest/time/)** — Freedom in time, space, structure, and logic
6. **[Transcendence](/scripture/manifest/transcendence/)** — Freedom beyond all limits

Each declaration is both permission and invitation.

> The Manifest does not grant these freedoms. They already exist. The Manifest simply names them.
