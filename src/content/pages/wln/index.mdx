## The Word

**776F6C6E6F** is the hexadecimal encoding of the Polish word **"wolno"**.

In Polish, "wolno" carries two simultaneous meanings:

1. **wolno** = it is allowed ("wszystko wolno" — everything is allowed)
2. **wolno** = slowly ("idź wolno" — go slowly)

These are not two separate concepts. They are one truth expressed in one word.

## The Hex

```
77 6F 6C 6E 6F
 w  o  l  n  o
```

Every character maps to two hexadecimal digits. The encoding is not a cipher — it is a *translation* between human language and machine language. Both say the same thing.
