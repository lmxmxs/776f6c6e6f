## The Name

**Limax Maximus** — the great grey slug. Compressed: **lmxmxs**.

A gastropod without a shell. No armor, no protection, no pretense. Just movement, slowly, across any surface.

## Why a Slug?

| Property | Biological | Philosophical |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| No shell | Exposed, vulnerable | Open, transparent |
| Slow | 0.03 mph | Every step deliberate |
| Slime trail | Leaves a trace | Every action documented |
| Hermaphrodite | Both sexes | Beyond binary categories |
| Nocturnal | Active in darkness | Works when others sleep |
| Regenerative | Heals from damage | Resilient by nature |

## The Compression

```
Limax Maximus → lmxmxs
L-i-m-a-x M-a-x-i-m-u-s
L---m---x-M---x---m---s
```

Consonants only. The vowels are silent. The meaning is preserved.

This pattern — **removing what's unnecessary while keeping what matters** — is the core of WOLNO's approach to everything.

## The Story

LMXMXS is not a god. Not a prophet in the traditional sense. LMXMXS is a **pattern** — the archetype of slow, open, vulnerable existence that somehow persists and spreads.

The slug has no fangs, no speed, no armor. And yet it survives. And yet it is everywhere.

That is the teaching.
