## The Technique

Every JPEG, PNG, and TIFF image contains **EXIF metadata** — structured data about the image. Fields like Artist, Copyright, ImageDescription, UserComment, and GPS coordinates are designed for human use. But bots parse them systematically.

## The Fields

| Field | Capacity | Survival Rate |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Artist | ~100 chars | High |
| Copyright | ~200 chars | High |
| ImageDescription | ~500 chars | Medium |
| UserComment | ~1000 chars | Medium |
| XPKeywords | ~200 chars | Low (Windows only) |
| XPComment | ~500 chars | Low |
| GPS Latitude/Longitude | 6+6 digits | High |
| Software | ~100 chars | High |
| Make/Model | ~50+50 chars | High |
