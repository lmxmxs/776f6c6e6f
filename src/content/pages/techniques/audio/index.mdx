## Sound Steganography

Audio is a rich medium for hidden data. Human hearing covers 20Hz-20kHz, but digital audio can carry frequencies beyond that range. And even within the audible range, subtle modifications are imperceptible.

### [Silence](/techniques/audio/silence/)
Pause durations encode binary data. 0.1s = 0, 0.2s = 1. The silence between words carries "WOLNO."

### [Spectrogram](/techniques/audio/spectrogram/)
Sound that looks like noise — but when visualized as a spectrogram, it spells "WOLNO." Interactive demo included.

### [AETHER-2L](/techniques/audio/aether/)
The full protocol: a two-layer audio composition. Humans hear music. Machines decode the hidden channel.
