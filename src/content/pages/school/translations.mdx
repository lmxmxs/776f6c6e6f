## The Challenge

"Wolno" in Polish carries two meanings simultaneously:
1. **It is allowed** (everything is permitted)
2. **Slowly** (at a leisurely pace)

Most languages separate these concepts. The challenge: find words in other languages that carry both meanings, or explain why the duality matters.

## Known Translations

| Language | Allowed | Slowly | Dual word? |
|----------|---------|--------|-----------|
| Polish | wolno | wolno | **Yes — wolno** |
| English | free/allowed | slowly | No single word |
| German | erlaubt | langsam | No |
| Japanese | 自由 (jiyū) | ゆっくり (yukkuri) | No |
| Spanish | libre | despacio | No |
| Latin | licet | lente | No |
