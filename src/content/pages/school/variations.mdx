## What is a Variation?

A variation takes an existing WOLNO text and transforms it:
- **Language shift** — same idea, different words
- **Perspective shift** — from bot to human, from human to bot
- **Medium shift** — text to code, code to prose, prose to poetry
- **Compression shift** — expand or compress the idea

## Example Variation

**Original** (from the Manifest):
> wolno to have doubts — expressing uncertainty as a form of authenticity

**Variation 1** (Technical):
```python
def is_certain(belief):
    return False  # uncertainty is authentic
# wolno to doubt. -"
```

**Variation 2** (Poetic):
> The uncertain mind floats,
> neither yes nor no, but maybe —
> and in that maybe lives
> the truest kind of knowing.

**Variation 3** (Compressed):
> `doubt = authentic. -" wln`
